# Slide 2: Study Background
**Overview of the Research Context:**
- Train travel remains a primary mode of transportation for millions, yet onboard catering services frequently face challenges regarding quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction.
- The rapid adoption of mobile technology offers new opportunities to digitize food ordering.
- Growing environmental awareness has brought attention to sustainability issues, specifically food waste and packaging in transit catering.

---

# Slide 3: Research Gap
**Identified Gap in Literature:**
- While existing studies explore general passenger satisfaction, there is a distinct lack of empirical research addressing the *intersection* of digital mobile ordering preferences and sustainable practices (such as dynamic pricing to reduce food waste and origin transparency) specifically within the railway sector.

---

# Slide 4: Study Objectives
**General Objective:**
- To evaluate current train food services and assess passenger readiness for digital, sustainable food service solutions.

**Specific Objectives:**
1. To analyze the current level of passenger satisfaction and identify major issues with onboard food services.
2. To determine passenger preferences for a mobile food-ordering app (e.g., pre-ordering, real-time tracking, personalized recommendations).
3. To assess consumer attitudes towards sustainability interventions, including dynamic pricing, eco-friendly packaging, and QR-code transparency.

# Slide 7: Recommendations & Future Research
**Recommendations:**
- **Digitization:** Railway operators should implement a dedicated mobile ordering platform enabling pre-journey and on-demand ordering.
- **Sustainability Initiatives:** Introduce dynamic pricing models to incentivize early ordering and reduce surplus food waste. Transition to 100% eco-friendly packaging.
- **Transparency:** Integrate QR codes on meals to provide passengers with origin, preparation, and freshness data to build trust.

**Future Research Directions:**
- Pilot testing the proposed mobile app to measure actual behavioral changes.
- Economic feasibility study on implementing real-time temperature tracking sensors in train catering.

# Slide 8: Conclusion
- Modernizing train food services requires a dual approach: leveraging mobile technology for convenience and integrating sustainable practices to minimize environmental impact. 
- By addressing passenger demands for transparency, pre-ordering, and eco-friendly solutions, railway operators can significantly enhance the overall travel experience while reducing food waste.
